Hey! Glad you're checking out my site. My name is Brandon El Haj and I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Portland, OR. I think making websites and apps is an art form of endless exploration and creativity that amazingly enough you get to share with others. This love of craft is what led me to start a small web design firm dedicated to helping small businesses with their web presences and brands. I believe web design is a vehicle for creating a better world, one great website at a time. I thoroughly enjoy creating unique user experiences combined with incredible aesthetics to make the internet a better place. I recently enrolled in Thinkful's Night's and Weekend program to further my education with the back-end. It's been great tackling problems and finding solutions with an awesome cohort of Web Developers. The paired programming has taught me the excitement of collaboration and how to be a good team player. When I'm not sitting at my computer you can find me playing soul music on my bass, exploring new restaurants in Portland, or walking my dog Rod.

Description:

The Mac HotKey Quiz app is built for the Mac user who wants to test their HotKey knowledge and learn a few new tricks. The quiz runs through the most important HotKeys for operating your Mac computer. At the end of the quiz, the user receives their score and determines whether or not they need to study more.
