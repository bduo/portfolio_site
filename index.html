Description:

The Mac HotKey Quiz app is built for the Mac user who wants to test their HotKey knowledge and learn a few new tricks. The quiz runs through the most important HotKeys for operating your Mac computer. At the end of the quiz the user sees their grade and determines whether or not they need to study more.
