Know Your Gov

Description:

Know Your Gov is a civic informational tool where users can look up their elected representatives at the state and federal levels using a home address. The full stack application utilizes the Google Civic Information API to deliver the elected representatives contact information and personal website. The responsive mobile first design allows users to easily contact their representative via phone, email, or through their website. Users also have the option of registering for the service and storing their address in the database to retrieve their representatives information for future reference.

Description:

The Community Toolbox is an online tool library where users can find and rent tools for home improvement projects. The full stack application allows the user to create a profile for reserving tools and checking them out. TripSpiration

Description:

This application relies on four APIs (FourSquare, OpenWeatherMap, Pixabay, Dark Sky) to inspire the user to travel to a particular city. The content is dynamically generated based on user input. When prompted to enter a travel destination they're curious about and their preferred travel month, the application returns a gallery of spectacular vistas of the city, current and projected weather conditions based on historical weather patterns, in addition to a list of popular local points of interest to visit. TripSpiration is here to help inspire your next trip!
